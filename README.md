# C3 RAG Assistant

This project implements a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system designed to support research and knowledge management at the Centro de Ciencias de la Complejidad (C3, UNAM).

The system combines a structured knowledge vault with semantic search and large language models to provide context-aware responses grounded in institutional and domain-specific information.

## Objectives

- Facilitate access to internal research materials, documents, and datasets
- Provide context-aware assistance for research questions
- Maintain continuity across interactions without losing relevant context
- Enable scalable knowledge integration over time

## Core Components

- **Knowledge Vault**  
  Structured storage of documents, notes, and curated information

- **Semantic Search Engine**  
  Embedding-based retrieval of relevant documents and fragments

- **RAG Pipeline**  
  Integration of retrieved context with LLM-generated responses

- **Context Management**  
  Mechanisms to preserve relevant conversational and domain context

## Use Cases

- Research assistance and literature exploration
- Internal documentation search
- Project knowledge consolidation
- Support for interdisciplinary collaboration

## Status

Work in progress. Initial focus is on building the core RAG pipeline and integrating a scalable knowledge vault.
